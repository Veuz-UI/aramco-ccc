The Cybersecurity Compliance Certification (CCC) program requires all third parties to obtain a compliance certificate from an authorized audit firm, ensuring adherence to Saudi Aramco’s Third Party Cybersecurity Standard (SACS-002) for conducting business.

The CCC program requires third parties to self-assess compliance against SACS-002 controls and have their assessment validated remotely by an authorized audit firm.

The CCC+ program mandates an onsite assessment by an authorized firm for third parties classified as Network Connectivity or Critical Data Processors. All other third parties must comply with CCC requirements.

Your Saudi Aramco proponent or contract owner will classify your company based on the Third Party Cybersecurity Standard (SACS-002). This classification determines whether you need a CCC or CCC+ certification to meet compliance requirements.
